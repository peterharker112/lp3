Our Story

Since 2015, White Pet Care has been delivering quality pet care with love and care. It began as a modest, unassuming project dedicated to making animals happy and healthy, and today it is the name pet owners know. Grooming, training, walking, and boarding services are just a few of the many ways we’ve developed to meet the different needs of pets and families over the years.

Our Mission

To serve pets at the highest level and offer owners peace of mind by offering professional, loving, and honest care.
